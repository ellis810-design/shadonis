# Shadonis readings — app source

Copy is drawn from **Astrocartography — Interpreted by Shadonis** (see `content/astrocartography-ebook.md` for the full ebook).

Edit this file, then run `npm run readings:sync`.

### Format

Each block: `## planet · angle` (lowercase planet + mc, ic, asc, dsc), then exactly these subsections:

`### Short theme` · `### What it feels like` · `### Best use cases` · `### Watch outs`

---

## sun · asc

### Short theme

Where your light meets the room

### What it feels like

This line is usually focused on your impact on others, how they view you and how you can use your image to your advantage. The Sun illuminates what is fact about who you are — AC territory is how you pierce a space and how others read you at face value.

### Best use cases

Personal brand, first impressions, visibility, business or following where attraction to you matters, stepping into how you want to be seen.

### Watch outs

Image and performance can overshadow quieter inner work; not everyone’s read of you is within your control.

## sun · ic

### Short theme

Grounded in who you are and what you build

### What it feels like

A line great for owning land and feeling extremely grounded in who you are and what you want to build. IC lines often feel like home, familiar, with room for belonging and long-term roots.

### Best use cases

Buying land, long-term home base, emotional grounding, building something lasting from a sense of inner truth.

### Watch outs

Comfort and roots can make it harder to stretch outward when life asks you to grow beyond the nest.

## sun · dsc

### Short theme

Support, trust, and company find you

### What it feels like

Energy where everyone seems to be assistive to you. Numerous acquaintances and the ability to trust and rely on others is highly likely. DC is where we fine-tune partnership and what we’re drawn to in others.

### Best use cases

Collaboration, alliances, finding people who show up for you, deepening trust in one-to-one and group bonds.

### Watch outs

Leaning on others is beautiful; keep a clear sense of self so partnerships stay mutual.

## sun · mc

### Short theme

Dreams, success, main-character drive

### Watch outs
