## Astrocartography Defined

Astrocartography is the study of the earth's energy in relation to your birth chart. It's your relationship with the planet you reside on.

Often times when we look at our birth chart, we're looking at a screen shot of the sky and what's above us. But, Astrocartography explains how these energies apply to us here on earth.

There's a lot to cover about how we learn the world around us. Through the people we meet, the books we read, the music we listen to, the artists we love, the films we watch etc. It's all connected and it's tied to our position and perspective on the planet.

By understanding key things in your birth chart, you can find your place in the world and how to make certain places work in your favor.

## Reading an Astrocartography Map

When you first spot an Astrocartography map, you'll notice lines of all colors. These lines indicate a planet and an angle from your chart.

The planet symbols indicate which planet and there are 4 different angles to pay attention to.

---

## The Four Angles

### AC (Ascendant)

The ascendant is also known as the "rising" sign is what I like to call the front lawn of your chart. It's what's on display and it's out of our control how others interpret our front lawn.

The ascendant helps us understand how we pierce a room with our energy when we walk into it and also how others perceive us at face value.

In astrocartography, the AC lines are places where we can focus on WHY people are drawn to us. If you have a business or some sort of following, often times AC lines will hold where you may have more attraction. AC lines are also likely to be associated with your effect on others in general.

### DC (Descendant)

The Descendant represents our understanding of partnership and alliances of all kinds. It's assigned to the 7th house, so it's how we understand what we are drawn to and why.

In Astrocartography, often times our DC lines are places where we have always been drawn to. It's where we are able to fine tune what we want in partnership and even where we may find a partner. Either way, it's always somewhere we'll find company eventually.

### IC (Imum Coeli)

The Imum Coeli stands as our grounding point. It's assigned to our 4th house so it's our tie to our upbringing, home life and how we define security, comfort and privacy.

In Astrocartography, IC lines often tend to be places that feel like "home" or extremely familiar. There can be this sense of belonging as well as ancestral ties. It's helpful to look to your IC lines to see where you could reside long term or own land.

### MC (Mid-Heaven)

The Mid-Heaven is the highest point in any birth chart and represents our understanding of legacy, career, public life, reputation and overall life goals. It's assigned to matters of the 10th house and helps us understand what we want to give to the world and what we want to be known for in the end.

In Astrocartography, often times MC lines can be places we feel inspired, driven and opportunities surrounding career can be plentiful. Keep in mind, acquiring accomplishments can also become the main focal point.

Once you gather the understanding of the specific angles of your chart and how they apply, you're closer to understanding your Astrocartography.

## The Lines

The lines in an Astrocartography map aren't just different colors but they represent the planetary orb distance that each one covers. You can expect a line to apply up to 700 miles in radius east or west, although west is known to be more favorable.

You will also notice patterns of different lines that may even cross and these are called parans. It's important to pay attention to when lines interact with each other as well as the latitude of each because they add another blended layer of information.

---

## Main Points / Zenith Points

Zenith points aka Main Points are the circles you will spot in an Astrocartography Map. Like the lines, they hold the energy of the planetary orb but now that energy is multiplied by 3 covering up to 3,000 miles in radius east or west.

These special points can be tied to a specific pull a location has on you or ancestral ties. Often times, Main Points can be places where we have pivotal experiences in life.

## Relocation Charts

After becoming familiar with your natal planetary positions, you can then add another layer of information by including your relocation chart for clarity.

The relocation chart is changing your birth location to the location of interest and often times, this can switch certain placements to help understand further how a location would specifically apply to you over time.

For example, in a birth chart, a person is a Gemini rising in their hometown of Georgetown, Guyana with their Moon in the 9th house trine their Pluto in the 5th. However if we change their birth chart to London, their rising changes to Cancer. Their Moon remains in the 9th house but their Jupiter, Chiron, Venus and 7th house stellium shift completely.

One location being a major focus on their relationships, to the other location being more focused on their ability to structure their life and focus on what they need spiritually and personally. This is valuable information if they were considering relocating to London.

Relocation charts are extremely helpful if you are considering long term stay but remember only significant changes will occur in a relocation chart if the location in question is over 200 miles further in distance.

## What Can Each Planet Represent in Relation to the Angles of Your Chart?

Although there is nothing general about Astrocartography, there are a few themes planetary lines can have and I've noticed similarities in majority of the charts I've studied.

---

### Sun Lines

The Sun illuminates what is fact about who we are and what we stand for in our chart. Sun energy is self focused because it asks us to reflect and face who we are. Sun lines are great for self awareness and tapping into what makes us special.

- **SUN AC** — This line is usually focused on your impact on others, how they view you and how you can use your image to your advantage.
- **SUN IC** — A line great for owning land and feeling extremely grounded in who you are and what you want to build.
- **SUN DC** — Energy where everyone seems to be assistive to you. Numerous acquaintances and the ability to trust and rely on others is highly likely.
- **SUN MC** — A line known for making dreams come true. A heavy focus on success and how you define it as an individual. Main character energy is also strong here.

---

### Moon Lines

The Moon governs what is undeniable about how we feel. It represents in our chart, how we emotionally process the world within and around us. Moon lines can help us sort through our emotions and help us to focus on whatever we have been suppressing over time. Subconscious patterns are also revealed often.

- **MOON AC** — You will feel the need to connect with others.
- **MOON IC** — A line that can feel long term, comfortable and stable.
- **MOON DC** — This line is great for intimacy and understanding our intricate needs in partnership.
- **MOON MC** — A lot of focus on what you need to come to terms with in order to achieve your definition of success and alignment.

---

### Mercury Lines

Mercury is not just about how we speak, it's how we think. Mercury is associated with communication, intelligence, learning and agility of the mind. Mercury lines can be chaotic but active in the way we approach ideas and others around us.

- **MERCURY AC** — A very social line perfect for school, sharing specific information perfectly and meeting new people consistently.
- **MERCURY IC** — Ideal for teaching and also adapting to new information long term.
- **MERCURY DC** — Another social line that supports connection with others and meeting of like minds.
- **MERCURY MC** — Perfect for long term education or completing a project that requires multiple people.

---

### Venus Lines

Venus represents what we want, what feels good and how we indulge. Venus lines can help us slow down and enjoy what we have, what we've worked hard for and can help us fine tune what we desire for our future.

- **VENUS AC** — Comfortability in the way you express yourself and connect with others. Another great line for vacation as well.
- **VENUS IC** — A comfortable and cozy environment that can immediately feel like home.
- **VENUS DC** — An energy that aids having long term partnerships of all kinds.
- **VENUS MC** — Great for career growth and overall Venusian themed goals.

---

### Mars Lines

Mars rules action, direction and how we take initiative. Although sometimes hyper active, the restlessness that comes with Mars lines can help us focus and become more aware of what we are here to do.

- **MARS AC** — Those drawn to you may be led by the way you take action and will be heavily impacted by it. Leadership positions are also likely.
- **MARS IC** — Many sharp realizations about life and personal matters.
- **MARS DC** — Friction toward others may occur often. Many conflicts can also take place along with personal injury.
- **MARS MC** — This is definitely one of those lines to pay attention to your Mars placement in specific. Mars MC lines can put you in extremely competitive circumstances.

---

### Jupiter Lines

Where ever Jupiter is in our chart, sheds light on what has a major influence within or around us. Jupiter lines help us understand what places make us feel like we can do, be and have MORE.

- **JUPITER AC** — A lot of opportunities to be noticed and a lot of people being drawn to who you are or what you do.
- **JUPITER IC** — Favorable line for vacation and enjoyment, a feeling to return is common.
- **JUPITER DC** — Extremely easy to connect with others almost to the point of never being alone.
- **JUPITER MC** — Many ties to popularity and fame shining light on who you are or what you do.

---

### Saturn Lines

Saturn represents diligence, time, karma, hard work and longevity. Often times where Saturn is in our chart is where we have many lessons to learn. Saturn lines help us embrace endurance and what it takes to stand the test of time.

- **SATURN AC** — You can teach a lot to those surrounding you in these areas.
- **SATURN IC** — Ties to ancestry, owning land as well as places we feel extremely bound to.
- **SATURN DC** — Long term bonds and partnerships can be formed.
- **SATURN MC** — Finding mentors and accomplishing long term goals is very likely.

---

### Uranus Lines

Uranus rules what we don't see coming. It's about change and the element of surprise. Although unstable, these lines can be an inspiring jolt of change and redirection helping you to hone in on your independence and stand out where you are.

- **URANUS AC** — Many unexpected encounters that can lead to changes you didn't see coming.
- **URANUS IC** — An unstable line for long term but significant shifts can take place in terms of grounding.
- **URANUS DC** — Same with AC energy only most of the encounters will be of those you're drawn to.
- **URANUS MC** — Great for finding personal freedom and harnessing individual expression.

---

### Neptune Lines

Neptune lines as a whole are engulfing. Because Neptune rules dreams and how we cope with reality, it can be very easy to vacation on them. Losing your grip on reality can also be a theme.

- **NEPTUNE AC** — Your approach when it comes to others may not be clear and it can be hard to have lasting connections.
- **NEPTUNE IC** — Usually a conflict with stability or feeling clear about long term stay.
- **NEPTUNE DC** — Great for short term experiences with people you may not know for a long time.
- **NEPTUNE MC** — A setting perfect for drifting off and being okay with not taking anything serious. A lot of long term dreams you've always had can be the focal point.

---

### Pluto Lines

Pluto represents transformation, power and control. Where it is in our charts is how we encompass these traits of life and how we apply it. Pluto lines are known for extreme transformation where Pluto rests in our charts.

- **PLUTO AC** — Experiences around major shifts in the way you learn, adapt and relate to others.
- **PLUTO IC** — Great location to reset, transform and collect yourself in every way.
- **PLUTO DC** — Can be challenges surrounding how you communicate with others, especially with those closest to you.
- **PLUTO MC** — Very potent, magnetic and transformative when it comes to what you want overall in life.

---

### North Node Lines

I love to call Lunar Node lines the "yellow brick road" to our destiny. The energy on any North Node line can provide extreme clarity and consistent epiphanies surrounding your destiny.

Something to notice, is how you feel after any line but especially after a North Node line.

- **NN AC** — Destiny ties to who is drawn to you.
- **NN IC** — Destiny and ancestral ties.
- **NN DC** — Destiny ties to who you are drawn to.
- **NN MC** — Extremely transformative and spiritually shifting in infinite ways. *(This line is the reason I study Astrocartography.)*
